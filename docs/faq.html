FAQ

How do I start using the service?
Just follow the link — the Telegram bot will open. Tap the "Start" button and you're ready to meet new people.
Do I need to install an app?
No. If you already have Telegram, you're all set. No extra apps required.
Is it free?
Yes, the dating service is free to use. Optional premium features are available.
Can others see my phone number?
No. Your phone number and any contact details are completely hidden from other users.
Are profiles verified?
Yes. We use AI to analyze profiles, and our moderation team manually reviews every submission to ensure quality and safety.
Are there any age restrictions?
Our service is available to users aged 21 and older.
What should I do if the bot doesn't respond?
If something isn’t working, contact our support team — we’ll help you as soon as possible.
